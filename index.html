Hi, my name is Markus Hecker. I am a student at the Ruhr-University Bochum in germany during my masters degree in IT-Security. I love making games in my freetime since I was 13 what is probably the reason I learned programming in the first place. Making a game for everyone to play and enjoy is my dream since but I never got serious enough to really publish anything besides very small projects with a dear colleege and friend of mine while attanding a game development course in unity at university. I want this to change with my game Tavern Traveler and want to take you along with me and my journey. This devlog is for you and me to read how I make my game from start to finish and how its core features work even if I had a longer break so I can read how certain things work.


I make most of the stuff for my games myself including textures, music and sounds. If you want to play my games I made in unity you can do so here on simmer.io.
