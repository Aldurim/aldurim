Hi, my name is Markus Hecker. I am a student at the Ruhr-University Bochum in germany during my master's degree in IT-Security. Since I was 13 I love making games in my free time what's probably the reason I learned programming in the first place. Making a game for everyone to play and enjoy had become a long dream of mine, but I never got serious enough to really publish anything besides very small projects with a dear colleague and friend of mine while attending a game development course in unity at university. I want this to change with my game Tavern Traveler and I want to take you along my journey.

This dev log is for you and me to read how I make my game from start to finish and how its core features work even if I had a longer break, so I can remember how certain things work.


I make most of the stuff for my games myself including graphics, music and sounds. If you want to play my games I made in unity you can do so here on simmer.io.
